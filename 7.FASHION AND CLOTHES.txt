  Speaking part 1 

1.  Do you enjoy buying clothes? 
2.  What type of clothes do you like to wear? 
3.  Do you wear different styles of clothes now compared  
    to 10 years ago? 
4.  Are you willing to wear a uniform at work? 
5.  What types of clothes do people in your local area  
    enjoy wearing? 
6.  Where do you buy most of your clothes? 
Speaking part 2 

 Describe someone you know who dresses well.  
 You should say 
 •  who they are 
 •  how you know them 
 •  what kind of clothes they wear 
 and say why you like the way they dress.
